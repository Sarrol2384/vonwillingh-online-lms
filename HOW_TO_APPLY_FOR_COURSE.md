# 🎓 HOW TO APPLY FOR A COURSE - Student Guide

## 🎯 You Want to Apply for a Course

There are **two ways** to apply:

---

## ✅ **METHOD 1: Student Portal (Recommended)**

### **Step 1: Go to Student Login**
```
https://vonwillingh-online-lms.pages.dev/student-login
```

### **Step 2: Create Account (if new student)**
If you don't have an account:
1. Click **"Register"** or **"Create Account"**
2. Fill in your details:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Password
   - Phone (optional)
3. Click **"Register"**
4. ✅ Account created!

### **Step 3: Login**
- Email: your email
- Password: your password
- Click **"Login"**

### **Step 4: Browse Courses**
Once logged in, you'll see:
```
Student Dashboard
├── 📚 Available Courses
├── 📝 My Applications
├── 🎓 My Enrollments
└── 📜 My Certificates
```

### **Step 5: View Available Courses**
You should see courses like:
- AI Basics for SA Small Business Owners (Certificate, R0.01)
- Excel Basics for Business Owners (Certificate, R2,500)
- Leadership Skills for Small Business Owners (Certificate, R2,600)
- And more...

### **Step 6: Apply for a Course**
1. Click on the course you want
2. Read the course details:
   - Level
   - Duration
   - Price
   - Modules
   - Description
3. Click **"Apply for This Course"** button
4. Fill in application form (if required)
5. Submit application
6. ✅ Application submitted!

### **Step 7: Wait for Approval**
- Admin will review your application
- You'll see status: "Pending" → "Approved" or "Rejected"
- Once approved, you can start learning!

---

## ✅ **METHOD 2: Direct Course View (No Login Required)**

### **Step 1: View All Courses**
```
https://vonwillingh-online-lms.pages.dev/courses
```

This shows all available courses publicly (no login needed)

### **Step 2: Browse Courses**
You'll see a list of courses with:
- Course name
- Level
- Category
- Price
- Duration
- Number of modules

### **Step 3: Click on a Course**
Click to view full details including:
- Description
- Module list
- Prerequisites
- What you'll learn

### **Step 4: Apply**
- Click **"Apply Now"** or **"Enroll"**
- You'll be redirected to login/register
- Complete the application process
